DreamTeam

This desktop app helps organize your time with weekly and monthly to-do list views, customizable presets for repeating tasks assigned to days of the week, and intuitive drag-and-drop support to easily mark out your day!
Implemented using JavaFX, Gluon Scenebuilder, JDBC, and a MySQL database hosted with Clever Cloud. Github repo can be found here.
